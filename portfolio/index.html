Using Innovative Technology in the Classroom

This activity was used with a 12th grade Honors English class. This activity is designed to get students thinking about wider motifs within the play Macbeth, but the activity could easily be adapted to other long texts, such as novels. By exploring word frequency with Voyant, students were able to see the motif in context and make predictions about how the motif would progress through the play.

Creative Methods for Generating Discussion

This activity was used in a 12th Grade general preparation course. It gave students a collaborative method for thinking through Macbeth's progression as a character through his soliloquies. The students got to demonstrate their understanding through multiple possible means: answering analysis questions, picking out a musical soundtrack that reflects Macbeth's character, and reading the lines dramatically.

7th Grade Unit Plan - Flesh and Blood So Cheap

This unit contains a unit rationale, lesson plans, reflection, and markup to indicate modifications and additions that I would use in future iterations of the unit. The unit focuses on reading informational text, and exploring primary and secondary sources. The unit culminates in a protest poster to help students make connections between the Triangle Shirtwaist Factory Fire and current labor conditions.
